About Us

Pro Fleet Recruiting is a Springfield, IL-based truck driver recruiting agency dedicated to connecting professional CDL drivers with reputable U.S. trucking companies. Our focus is on helping drivers find stable, well-paid opportunities while supporting carriers with dependable, long-term talent.

With extensive knowledge of the U.S. trucking industry, we understand what drivers value most: fair pay, consistent miles, respect, and clear communication. We work closely with both drivers and employers to ensure every placement is the perfect match.

Whether you are an experienced CDL driver or exploring new career opportunities, we guide you through the hiring process from start to finish. At Pro Fleet Recruiting, your success on the road is our top priority.
